Pacman Recreation

This Pacman was created using a framework provided by Staffordshire University

Description

This was set as a project in University to see what we could do, with various tutorials helping me along the way.

What I Decided To Do Differently

I wanted to add something unusual to my game, because when you think multiplayer pacman you generally think of 2 pacmans or you play once then the next person has a go. I wanted to change this by enabling someone to control the ghost, to balance the game pacman can't circle from top to bottom only left to right this is to give the ghost a better chance of trying to get pacman.
